Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a beautiful country which can be found Central America it is along the coastlines on the Carribean and Pacific ocean. Its capital city is San José. It is the largest city that can be found in Costa Rica. This city can actually be found in the center of the country around the mid west of the central valley within San José Canton. Their capital city is a place with cultural institutions like the Pre-Columbian Gold Museum. Things that Costa Rica is known for is for its, beaches, volcanoes, and is biodiversity. Around a quarter of its area is actually made up of protected jungle. Its wildlife includes spider monkeys and quetzal birds. A lot of its economy thrives on tourism, agriculture, and more specifically coffee and bananas, and tech. To end this off we can see that with Costa Rica's welcoming culture, amazing landscapes, and strong commitment to both peace and environmental stewardship, the country stands out as a very unique and inspirning place.
