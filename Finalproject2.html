El Salvador

El Salvador is Central Americas most densely populated country, however it is the smallest. It is mostly known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and natural landscapes. It is bordered by Honduras and Guatemala, and its the only country without a Caribbean coastline. With that however it has a Pacific shoreline with great spots for surfing like El Tunco and La libertad. El Salvador has a beautiful diverse landscape which features volcanoes, mountains, as well as lakes. The capital city is San Salvador. Is has historical landarks, museums, and a growing modern infrastructure. Despite the challenges that El Salvador has faced it has made strides in economic development and security in the past recent years. In conclusion it is a country that is full of history, beautiful land, and a lot of potential.
